Welcome!

The mental health professionals associated with Breakthrough Family Therapy are dedicated to working together to support the health, happiness, and well-being of individuals, couples, and families in our community.

Therapists at Breakthrough Family Therapy take pride in working collaboratively with our clients, acknowledging that you are the expert regarding your life. We aim to establish and maintain a solid relationship with our clients based on respect and trust.

Our therapists are committed to ethical practice which is founded upon integrity, skilled-training, and continuing education in order to best support the well-being of our clients.

We strive to always maintain a multi-cultural awareness and therapy practice realizing that differences of gender, race, age, sexual orientation, and culture create unique issues that should not be minimized.
